How to install Microsoft Store Apps

  1. Open the App Center
  2. Click on an app
  3. When asked, click Open
  4. In the 'Windows protected your PC' dialogue, click More info
  5. Click Run anyway
  6. Click Install
  7. The app should now be available in the 'All apps' section of Start.
